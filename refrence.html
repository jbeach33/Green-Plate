At Green Plate, we are proud to share the idea that great, whole vegan food is for everyone. We aim to provide flavor and creativity in plant-based meals that nurture our bodies while at the same time inspiring our community to pick the fresher and healier option. We use only the best and freshest ingredients in each of our dishes, sourced from local vendors and farmers whenever possible, to assure the freshest and most sustainable produce is in each of our dishes. Each plate gets the passion and skill of our team of chefs put into making the meal as satisfying as it is nourishing. For our long time vegans or just those who would like to try something new, we can promise that each and every bite will leave you wanting more. Come visit us, and let the discovery begin with the real flavors of carefully prepared food bursting in perfectly crafted bite. At Green Plate, we believe that vegan food can be flavorful, fresh, and accessible to all and thats why we started this restaurants.
